About


Hello! My name is William, I am a UX designer & full-stack web developer who has been absorbed into the React library. I am well versed in many trades including music, massage therapy, and electrical work. Using both my creative and logical sides I aim to provide a user experience that's intimate and direct. Having found my passion through web development, I am highly skilled in writing well-designed, testable and efficient code using current best practices. Please enjoy my eclectic array of apps I have built for your pleasure, and feel free to contact me!
